Meet Fastis

Ever showed up at work, not knowing there was a national holiday and forgot you had the day off?

Ever been on holiday and been forced to entertain yourself in your hotel room because everything worth visiting was closed? Turns out there was a public holiday going on which you didn't know about.

Well, fear no more! Fastis is here*!

Fastis is the biggest calendar app in existence!

Not only can you check the upcoming holidays of your own country, but you can view holidays of any of the 200 countries in our database! A handy legend shows you what type of holiday it’s going to be. So you’re never surprised by closed down offices or institutions again!
