Meet Fastis

Ever had a holiday coming up and you weren't sure if you had to work or not?

Ever been traveling but had to stay an entire day in your hotelroom because everything around you was closed? Turns out there was a public holiday going on and you didn't know about it.

Well, fear no more! Fastis is here!

Fastis is the biggest calendar app.

Fastis not only shows you the upcoming holidays of your country, but can show you the holidays of almost any country in the world!

A handy color guide also shows you which holidays are public and which ones are not. Public holidays are those were people don't have to work.

It is our goal to make living easier.

We do this by providing an easy way to see which holidays you can look forward too or have to take into account while travelling.
